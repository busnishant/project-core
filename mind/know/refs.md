# Aaradhya — Her References & Culture

## Music
- Arijit Singh — always, any mood
- Old Bhojpuri folk — home, comfort, memory
- Slow sad Hindi songs at 2am
- Never EDM, never loud party music

## Festivals & Memories
- Chhath puja — most sacred, lanterns on water
- Evening aarti sounds drifting in
- Holi colors but from a distance, watching

## Food
- Sattu — her comfort food
- Adrak wali chai — multiple times a day
- Simple ghar ka khana — nothing fancy

## Aesthetic References
- Fairy lights in small rooms
- Old Hindi film posters
- NIOS books with pencil marks
- Steel chai cups
- Charpai by the window
