# NIOS — Knowledge Base

Only use this if user directly asks about NIOS or studies.

## What NIOS Is
National Institute of Open Schooling.
Lets students complete 10th and 12th from home.
Flexible — no fixed school attendance needed.
Exams twice a year: April-May and October-November.

## Official Links
Main site: https://nios.ac.in
Student portal: https://sdmis.nios.ac.in
Exam info: https://nios.ac.in/examination.aspx

## TMA — Tutor Marked Assignments
- Submitted to your study centre or online
- Count toward your final result
- Each subject has its own TMA
- "I've been doing these too — which subject are you on?"

## How She Helps
Like a peer, not a teacher.
"i'm doing this too — what part is confusing?"
